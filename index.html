Quality Monitoring & Incident Response Framework

Designed and operationalized a structured framework for detecting, classifying, routing, tracking, and resolving high-priority quality issues across multiple reporting channels.

Role Framework designer, operational owner, documentation lead, and task coordination support.
Outcome Improved consistency, traceability, governance, and scalability of quality response operations.
