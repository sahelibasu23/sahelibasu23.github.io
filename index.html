Flagship professional system

Quality Monitoring & Incident Response Framework

Designed and operationalized a structured framework for detecting, classifying, routing, tracking, and resolving high-priority quality issues across multiple reporting channels, with AI-assisted support used for preliminary triage, multi-source interpretation, workflow testing, and assignment preparation.

Problem Fragmented reporting, inconsistent priority handling, unclear ownership, and limited operational visibility.
Approach Created a unified issue model, severity language, routing workflow, documentation, and operating cadence, supported by AI-assisted analysis where useful.
Role Framework designer, operational owner, documentation lead, and task coordination support, and AI-enabled workflow optimizer.
AI Support Used LLM-assisted triage, preliminary analysis of multiple data sources, outcome interpretation, assignment-card preparation, and workflow testing.
Outcome Improved consistency, traceability, governance, response coordination, and scalability of quality operations.
